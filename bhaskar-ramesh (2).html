Bhaskar
Ramesh
Director - Tech, FMCG, Auto, Media & Entertainment
Google

Bhaskar heads YouTube and runs the Brand business for India. He is also recognized as among ‘Most influential Marketing Leaders 2017’ by CMO Asia & World Marketing Congress. He is a passionate marketer & business leader with 15+ years of experience across CPG, Media & Entertainment and Technology Industries in India. He is specialized in advising CXOs on marketing in digital world & how to transform their business in an increasingly Video First India.
